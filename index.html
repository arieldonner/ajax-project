About Gamer'sCodex

Welcome to Your Gamer Codex, where you can save all of those links and guides to games that are taking up browser space and add personal notes. Below are some tips for using the site.

1. Click on Games to view currently featured games on Steam. Use the search bar to search for a specific game that you are playing.

2. Once you found a game, add it to MyCodex by clicking on the heart icon next to the game name.

3. View your saved games in MyCodex. In MyCodex, you can add and edit notes for your games. Just click on the tile for the game you want to edit.

4. Once you have added your notes, click Submit and you can come back to them again at any time.

5. Enjoy Your Codex and a cleaner web browser.
